Our Distinctives

Many claim to offer quality coffee, but Greenridge Coffee has objective evidence to back it up. Here's what sets us apart.

Cup of Excellence Caliber Microlots

Greenridge Coffee comes from various smallholder farms, depending on supply chain. While they're all top notch, one is a former winner in the Cup of Excellence: a prestigious international competition.

Single origin Ethiopian Coffee

Ethiopia's high-altitude regions benefit from diverse microclimates and are renowned for producing coffee with nuanced flavor profiles.

Dedication to Quality Processing

Each step of our harvesting process is strategic, from small batch handpicking for ripeness to drying beans to precise moisture percentages.

Personalized Roast & Brewing Guidance

Every order includes a guide to simplify your roasting experience. Plus, our virtual Greenridge Coffee expert, powered by ChatGPT, is trained to offer personalized advice tuned to your equipment and taste preferences.

Fairer than Fair Trade Sourcing

Our source farms receive significantly more compensation than the standard "Fair Trade" minimum, fairly rewarding the superior quality they produce.

Selfless Customer Support

We at Greenridge Coffee prioritize customer satisfaction above all else. We will go beyond the expected to do what is best for you, every time.
